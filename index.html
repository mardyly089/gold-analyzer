How this analysis was calculated
Direction (UP/DOWN/NEUTRAL) — price vs. session open (00:00 UTC).
Momentum bar — % change from session open; capped at ±2% for display scale.
Zones — session open, high, and low, plus nearest round-number levels.
Trade setup — entry within 0.3% of zone; SL beyond zone; TP at 1:3+ RR.
Bull/Bear scenarios — triggered by break of session high or low respectively.
